-----------------------------------

Customer Engagement and Personalization
-------------------------------------------

Retailers can also use AI-based recommendation systems to offer relevant product suggestions to each customer, based on their buying behavior, browsing history, and product preferences.

--------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------
