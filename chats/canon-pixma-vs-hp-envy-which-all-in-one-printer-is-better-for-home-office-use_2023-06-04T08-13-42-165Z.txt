**Canon PIXMA vs. HP Envy: Features**

Both Canon PIXMA and HP Envy are all-in-one printers, which means you get printing, scanning, copying, and faxing functionalities in one device. Let's compare their features.

***Printing Quality and Speed***

When it comes to printing quality, Canon PIXMA seems to be the winner due to its high resolution and print head technology. Regarding printing speeds, both printers are similar when it comes to black and white printing, but HP Envy is faster with color printing. Canon PIXMA takes around 15-20 seconds to print 4X6 print while HP Envy takes around 8-10 seconds on the same print size.

***Scanning***

The scanning quality of both printers is good, but Canon PIXMA produces slightly better results due to its high resolution up to 2400 x 4800 dpi. Canon PIXMA has an auto document feeder (ADF) that allows you to scan multiple pages automatically, which is useful when you have a lot of pages to scan. HP Envy doesn’t have an ADF, and you have to scan each page manually. 

***Copying***

When it comes to copying features, both printers have similar functionalities like reducing and enlarging the size of the document, making multiple copies at once, and adjusting the text/photo quality. However, copying speed is faster in the HP Envy than in the Canon PIXMA. 

***Connectivity***

Both printers have similar connectivity features like Wi-Fi, USB, and mobile printing. HP Envy has an extra feature of Wireless Direct Printing, which allows you to connect the printer directly to your smartphone or tablet without using a Wi-Fi network. The Canon PIXMA has an advantage over HP Envy when it comes to Cloud Printing. Canon PIXMA has built-in Cloud printing that can print from cloud services like Google Drive, OneDrive, and Dropbox.

**Canon PIXMA vs. HP Envy: Design and Build Quality**

When it comes to design, HP Envy has sleek looks and provides easy access to cartridges. The printer has a touch screen display that can be controlled with touch and swipe gestures. Canon PIXMA has slightly bulkier looks but provides similar functionalities like HP Envy. Canon PIXMA has two paper trays, while HP Envy has only one. 

Regarding build quality, both printers are equally durable and come with one-year limited warranty.

**Canon PIXMA vs. HP Envy: Price**

The price of both printers is almost the same, ranging from $100 - $200 depending on the model you choose. However, the ink cartridges of HP Envy are comparatively cheaper than Canon PIXMA.
