- Enhance supply chain visibility and traceability

These benefits translate into a more competitive manufacturing industry, with shorter lead times, greater customization, and higher quality products.

- Data security and privacy concerns
- Compatibility issues with legacy systems and protocols
- High implementation and maintenance costs
- Lack of skilled personnel with the necessary expertise

Addressing these challenges will require significant investments in IoT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and workforce training.

## IoT Trends in Manufacturing

- The development of new IoT-enabled products, such as wearables and smart appliances
- Increased collaboration and data-sharing between manufacturers and suppliers
