A CDN is a distributed network of servers that helps to speed up website loading times by caching content close to the user's location. Using a CDN can significantly reduce website load times, but how do you choose the best CDN for your website?

In this post, we'll explore the top 5 CDN performance benchmarking and comparison tools that can help you find the fastest CDN for your website.
