**3. Comfort** - Choose earbuds that fit comfortably in your ears and come with multiple ear tip sizes for a perfect fit.

**4. Connectivity** - Look for earbuds that support Bluetooth 5.0 for a reliable connection and stable audio playback.

**5. Price** - Set a price range that fits your budget and consider the features you want in your earbuds.
