1. Improved Worker Safety: Mining and extraction are inherently dangerous industries, and worker safety is of paramount importance. By creating immersive training scenarios, workers can gain hands-on experience in a safe and controlled environment. In summary, Augmented Reality is set to revolutionize the mining and extraction industry, delivering improved worker safety, increased productivity, and reduced costs.
