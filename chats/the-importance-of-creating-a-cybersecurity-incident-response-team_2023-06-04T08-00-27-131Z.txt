## What is a Cybersecurity Incident Response Team?
A cybersecurity incident response team (CIRT) is a group of experts that is dedicated to preventing and responding to cyber attacks. The team is responsible for identifying and containing any cybersecurity incidents that may occur within an organization. The team comprises of diverse experts such as cybersecurity analysts, IT personnel, legal representatives, communication experts, and management personnel. The main objective of a CIRT is to minimize the damage caused by a cyber attack. The team is also responsible for identifying and patching vulnerabilities in the organization's systems and networks that can be exploited by hackers.
