By connecting different modes of transport, such as buses, trains, and taxis, passengers can enjoy a more seamless travel experience, without the need for constant transfers.

In addition to connected technologies, the future of public transportation will also be heavily influenced by autonomous technologies. In addition to improving safety, autonomous vehicles can significantly reduce travel times by using advanced algorithms to optimize routes and avoid congestion. This could also lead to a reduction in the number of vehicles on the road, helping to ease traffic congestion and improve air quality.
