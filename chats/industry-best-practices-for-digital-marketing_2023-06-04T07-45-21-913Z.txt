Here are the top 7 industry best practices for successful digital marketing in 2021:

##1. Personalization
In today's market, personalization is key. Customers expect more than just a generic marketing message - they want personalized experiences that speak to their individual needs and interests. Marketers who can leverage data to segment their customer base and deliver personalized content and messaging will win big in 2021.

##2. Automation
Automation tools can help to streamline your digital marketing efforts and make the most of your time. From email campaigns to social media scheduling, there are countless options available that can help to reduce workload and improve efficiency.

##3. Video content
Video is quickly becoming the most popular form of online content. Whether it's short-form clips on social media or in-depth video series on YouTube, video content can help to engage your audience and deliver your message in a more engaging and memorable way.

##4. Influencer marketing
Influencer marketing has exploded in popularity in recent years, as more and more businesses look to tap into the power of social media influencers to reach new audiences and improve brand awareness. Identify influencers in your industry and leverage their reach to spread your message.

##5. Analytics
Data is the lifeblood of digital marketing. By analyzing key metrics and performance indicators, marketers can gain valuable insights into what's working and what's not, and adjust their strategies accordingly.

##6. Storytelling
Storytelling has always been a powerful marketing tool, and it's more important than ever in 2021. By crafting compelling stories that resonate with your target audience, you can create deeper connections and drive customer engagement in a way that generic marketing messages simply can't.
