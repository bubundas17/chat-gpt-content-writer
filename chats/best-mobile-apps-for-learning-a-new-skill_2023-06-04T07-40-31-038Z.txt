If you're looking to expand your horizons and learn a new skill but are struggling with a time commitment, mobile learning apps are the answer! Whether you want to learn a new language, develop your coding prowess or take up a new hobby, mobile learning apps can make all the difference. Here are some of the best mobile apps for learning a new skill.

### Duolingo
Duolingo is one of the best language learning apps in the market, which provides courses for 36 different languages. With Duolingo, you can learn languages like Spanish, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, and much more. The app comprises various exercises that enhance your listening, writing, reading, and speaking skills.

### Udemy
Udemy is one of the leading online learning platforms that offers a wide variety of courses in different fields like programming, business, marketing, health, music, and much more. Udemy has a mobile application through which you can get access to all the courses on the go. It provides you with the flexibility to learn at your own pace, access course materials like videos and articles on demand.

### SoloLearn
SoloLearn is an app that helps you learn coding on the go. The app comprises a vast library of interactive courses that makes coding simple and fun. You can learn various programming languages like HTML, CSS, Python, Java, and much more.

### SkillShare
SkillShare is another popular learning platform that provides users with online courses to enhance their skills in various fields like graphic design, photography, music composition, web development, and much more. SkillShare's mobile app offers users access to their library of courses and allows users to download courses to view offline.

### Lumosity
Lumosity is an app designed to enhance cognitive skills like memory retention, problem-solving, and attention span. It offers users a collection of 50+ games and challenges that test your cognitive abilities. The app tracks your progress and builds a customized set of games to help you improve in areas where you are l weak.

### Khan Academy
Khan Academy offers a wide variety of courses ranging from science and engineering to humanities and economics. The app offers over 10,000 educational videos covering a range of topics, including Math, Physics, and Biology, among others. The platform is highly interactive and includes interactive quizzes and exercises that help you test your knowledge.

### Coursera
Coursera is a renowned online learning platform that offers online courses from notable universities like Yale, Johns Hopkins, University of Pennsylvania, etc. The app has numerous courses in different fields, including business, computer science, humanities, health, and much more.

Whether you want to learn a new language, brush up your coding skills, or grow your business skills, these mobile learning apps make learning easily accessible and convenient. So, download these apps today and start your learning journey right away!
