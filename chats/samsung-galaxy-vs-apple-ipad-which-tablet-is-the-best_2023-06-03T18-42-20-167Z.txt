One major factor to consider when choosing a tablet is its design and build quality. The Samsung Galaxy tablets have a sleek design and are known for their lightweight build. The Galaxy Tab S7+, for instance, features a 12.4-inch AMOLED display, while the iPad Pro offers only a 12.9-inch LCD display. However, the iPad Pro has a faster refresh rate of 120Hz, which makes scrolling and animations smoother.

## Samsung Galaxy vs. Apple iPad: Performance

When it comes to performance, both Samsung Galaxy and Apple iPad offer powerful options. Samsung Galaxy runs on Android, while Apple iPad runs on iOS. Samsung's latest Galaxy tablets come with Snapdragon and Exynos processors, while the iPad Pro features the A12Z Bionic chip. The performance difference between the two is negligible and depends on individual use cases.

## Samsung Galaxy vs. Apple iPad: Battery Life

Battery life is another crucial feature to consider when picking a tablet. Samsung Galaxy and Apple iPad both offer decent battery life, but Samsung has an edge in this category. ## Samsung Galaxy vs. Apple iPad: Price

The price point of the device is another critical factor in choosing a tablet.
