Salesforce provides a cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) system that small businesses can use to manage their operations effectively. With Salesforce, small businesses can enhance their sales processes, track leads, and access customer information from anywhere, at any time.

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a comprehensive cloud computing platform that offers a range of services for small businesses. AWS is designed to be scalable, reliable, and cost-effective, which makes it a popular choice for startups and small to medium-sized businesses. AWS services include compute, storage, database, analytics, machine learning, and more.

**4. Google Cloud Platform**

Zoho is a comprehensive cloud-based business management platform that small businesses can leverage for enhanced operations.
