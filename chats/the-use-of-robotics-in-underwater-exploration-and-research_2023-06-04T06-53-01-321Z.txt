Additionally, robots are being used to study the degradation of the ocean, particularly in areas heavily impacted by human activity. This information allows scientists to make recommendations for protecting and preserving the ocean and the many species that call it home.

These machines have unlocked new realms of data and exploration that were previously inaccessible to humans.
