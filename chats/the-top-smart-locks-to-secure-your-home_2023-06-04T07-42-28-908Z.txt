The August Smart Lock Pro is an excellent choice if you're looking for a lock that's both affordable and easy to install. This smart lock boasts several amazing features at a reasonable price point.

The Yale Real Living Assure Smart Lock is one of the top smart locks that have been successfully integrated with popular home automation systems. One can remotely lock or unlock it using the Yale Secure app, Alexa, Google Assistant, Apple HomeKit and more.

Another Schlage lock that makes our top 5 list is the Schlage Sense. It is a great option for those who have an Apple HomeKit-enabled smart home since it works best when used with Siri voice commands.
