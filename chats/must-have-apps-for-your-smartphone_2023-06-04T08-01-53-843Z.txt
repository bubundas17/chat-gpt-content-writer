That's why we've put together this list of must-have apps for your smartphone in 2021. From productivity tools to entertainment options and everything in between, these apps will help you get the most out of your device and simplify your daily life.

1. Google Drive 
Google drive allows you to store, access and share files from anywhere, at any time. It's perfect for both personal and business use and can be used across multiple devices. Additionally, Google Drive comes with a built-in suite of apps, including Google Docs, Sheets and Slides, making it an all-in-one productivity suite. 

2. Spotify
If you're a music lover, then Spotify is the app for you. With access to millions of songs, curated playlists, and personalized recommendations, Spotify offers the ultimate listening experience. You can discover new artists, follow your favorite bands, and listen to podcasts.

3. Headspace 
In today's fast-paced world, we could all benefit from mindfulness and meditation. Headspace is a meditation app that teaches you how to meditate and improve your well-being. It offers a range of guided meditations tailored to your specific needs, including sleep, stress, and focus.

4. TripIt 
If you love to travel, then you need TripIt. This app organizes all of your travel information, including flight and hotel reservations, car rentals, and even restaurant reservations. The app creates a master itinerary that you can access anytime, anywhere, even offline.

5. MyFitnessPal 
MyFitnessPal is a fitness app that helps you track your daily calorie intake, exercise, and water consumption. It offers a vast database of foods and drinks, making it easier to log your meals and snacks accurately. Additionally, the app offers personalized recommendations and insights based on your goals.

6. Instagram 
Instagram is one of the most popular social media platforms worldwide and is designed for sharing photos and videos. It's perfect for staying in touch with friends and family, following your favorite celebrities, and discovering new brands and influencers.

7. Netflix 
Netflix is a video streaming app that offers an extensive library of TV shows, movies, and documentaries. You can watch your favorites anytime, anywhere, and even download them to watch offline. Additionally, Netflix offers personalized recommendations based on your viewing history.

8. Uber 
Uber is a ride-sharing app that connects you with a network of drivers for on-demand rides anywhere in the world. With the app, you can estimate fare costs, track your driver's location, and pay seamlessly through your smartphone. 

In conclusion, whether you want to improve your productivity, health, or entertainment options, these apps will help you achieve your goals in 2021 and beyond. They are must-haves for any smartphone user looking to simplify their daily life.
