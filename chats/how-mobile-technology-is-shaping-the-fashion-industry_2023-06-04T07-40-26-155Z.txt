Virtual try-on technology is another area where mobile technology is making waves in the fashion industry. With augmented reality (AR), consumers can virtually try on clothes and accessories, even in the comfort of their own homes. With virtual try-on technology, there is no longer a need to guess about the fit or to worry about returns.

Wearable technology is another area where fashion and mobile technology are intersecting. Wearable technology is also providing new opportunities for retailers to engage with shoppers.
