2. Create a Walkway:

Walkways can help improve the overall appearance of your garden and make it more accessible for visitors. Consider adding a stone or brick pathway that winds through your plants and flowers.

Choose plants and flowers that thrive in your climate and soil conditions. If you're unsure which plants to choose, visit your local nursery or garden center for advice.

7. Deadhead Regularly:
