Best Practices for Domain Name Management

Here are some best practices for managing your domain name:

1. Keep track of expiration dates: Keep track of when your domain name is set to expire. This will ensure that you renew it on time and avoid any disruptions to your online presence.

3. Use a reputable registrar: Ensure that you use a reputable registrar for your domain name. This will ensure that your domain name is secure and that you receive proper support when needed.
