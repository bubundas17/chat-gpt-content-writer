## Internet of Things (IoT)
Wi-Fi 6 also supports Internet of Things (IoT) devices, opening up new possibilities for automation and data collection. In healthcare, Wi-Fi-enabled wearables can monitor patients' vitals, allowing doctors to receive real-time updates on their conditions. In manufacturing, IoT sensors can track equipment performance, enabling proactive maintenance and reducing downtime. With Wi-Fi advancements, IoT devices can now operate at faster speeds and with lower power consumption.

## Wi-Fi Mesh Networks
Traditional Wi-Fi networks have limitations in terms of range and coverage. However, with Wi-Fi mesh networks, we can now extend the range and coverage of our wireless networks, making it easier to connect devices in large environments. In retail, for example, Wi-Fi mesh networks can improve customer experience by enabling location-based services and faster payment processing. In transportation, it can improve safety by providing faster communication between vehicles.
