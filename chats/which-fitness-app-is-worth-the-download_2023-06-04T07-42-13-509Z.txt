2. Nike Training Club: With over 185 free workouts and personalized training plans, Nike Training Club offers a variety of exercises ranging from strength training to yoga. Plus, the app continues to add new workout programs regularly.

3. Fitbit: Fitbit is a popular wearable device that tracks your daily activity, heart rate, and sleep. The app also allows you to log your food and water intake and connect with friends for a little friendly competition.

4. Sworkit: Short for “Simply Work It,” Sworkit offers customizable workout plans ranging from 5-60 minutes in length. Whether you want to focus on cardio, strength training, or yoga, Sworkit has a plan for you.

5. Zombies, Run!: If you’re looking for a fun and unique way to get your cardio in, look no further than Zombies, Run!. This app turns your daily jog or run into an immersive audio adventure where you’re trying to outrun a zombie apocalypse.

6. Aaptiv: With over 2,500 audio-guided workouts ranging from running to strength training, Aaptiv is perfect for those who enjoy a coach-led workout. Plus, the app allows you to filter workouts by length and difficulty level.

7. Fiit: Whether you want to join live or on-demand classes, Fiit delivers engaging and interactive workouts across a variety of disciplines like yoga, pilates, and barre. Plus, the app provides real-time heart rate monitoring for an extra boost of motivation.

8. 7 Minute Workout: For those who have limited time or are always on-the-go, 7 Minute Workout offers quick but effective workouts that can be done anywhere with no equipment required.

9. Peloton Digital: While Peloton is known for its high-end bikes and treadmills, the Peloton Digital app offers its users access to thousands of on-demand workouts without the need for expensive equipment. Plus, you can connect with other Peloton users for added accountability.

10. Strava: Perfect for runners and cyclists, Strava tracks your distance, pace, and elevation on your runs or rides. Plus, the app allows you to connect with friends and join challenges for added motivation.

In conclusion, these are our top 10 picks for fitness apps worth downloading in 2021. Whether you’re tracking your nutrition, building strength, or just trying to stay active, there’s an app on this list that can help you achieve your fitness goals. Happy downloading!
