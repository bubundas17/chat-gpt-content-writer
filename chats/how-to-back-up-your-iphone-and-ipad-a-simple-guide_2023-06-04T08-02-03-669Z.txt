2. Go to Settings on your iPhone or iPad

4. Make sure iCloud Backup is turned on.
5. Click Back Up Now.

That’s it! Your device will begin backing up immediately. You can also choose to have your device back up automatically if it’s connected to Wi-Fi and plugged in.

**Backing up Your iPhone and iPad Via iTunes**
iTunes can also be used to back up your iPhone and iPad. Here’s how:

1. Connect your device to your computer.
2. Open iTunes and select your iPhone or iPad.
3. Click Back Up Now.

4. Follow the steps to choose your backup and set up your device.
