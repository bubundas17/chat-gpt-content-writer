The average salary for a cloud engineer is $130,000 per year, with experienced cloud architects commanding salaries over $190,000 or more. User experience and user interface designers create intuitive and enjoyable user experiences across a range of digital platforms, from apps and websites to games and other interactive products. DevOps engineers are responsible for integrating development and operations teams to enable faster and more efficient software development processes. Product managers are responsible for leading cross-functional teams to develop and launch software products that meet the needs of customers and the business. They have a deep understanding of customer needs and market trends, and work closely with designers, engineers, and other stakeholders to ensure the product is delivered on time and within budget. The average salary for a product manager is $107,000 per year, with experienced product managers earning salaries of over $160,000 or more.

IT managers and directors are responsible for managing and directing the IT department of an organization, responsible for the planning, implementation, and maintenance of the organization's technology infrastructure. These professionals should have experience in software development, network administration, cybersecurity, or system administration. The average salary for an IT manager is $85,000 per year, with IT directors earning an average salary of $147,000 per year.

Full stack developers and back-end developers are in high demand as companies seek to create efficient, scalable, and secure applications and services. Full stack developers have experience in both front-end and back-end development, while back-end developers focus on creating the server-side applications and infrastructure that power digital experiences.
