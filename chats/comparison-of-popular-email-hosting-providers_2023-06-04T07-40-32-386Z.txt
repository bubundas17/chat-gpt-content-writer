In this post, we will compare some of the best email hosting providers in 2021 based on features, pricing, security and performance.

1. Google Workspace

Google Workspace, formerly known as G Suite, is a popular email hosting provider for businesses. It offers a suite of productivity tools, including Gmail, Google Drive, Google Docs and more. With Google Workspace, you can easily manage your emails, contacts and calendar from one place. Google Workspace offers excellent security features and provides 24/7 customer support. Prices start at $6 per user per month.

2. Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365, formerly known as Office 365, is another popular email hosting provider. It offers email hosting, file storage, and collaboration tools like Microsoft Teams. With Microsoft 365, you get access to Microsoft Outlook and can easily manage your emails, appointments and contacts. Microsoft 365 offers excellent security features and provides 24/7 customer support. Prices start at $5 per user per month.

3. Zoho Mail

Zoho Mail is a popular email hosting provider that offers a comprehensive suite of office tools along with email hosting. Zoho Mail offers easy integration with other Zoho applications such as Zoho CRM, Zoho Projects, and Zoho Docs. Zoho Mail also offers excellent security features such as two-factor authentication and email encryption. Prices start at $1 per user per month.

4. Rackspace

Rackspace is a leading email hosting provider that offers a range of email hosting options to businesses of all sizes. Rackspace provides excellent support for Microsoft Exchange, which is ideal for businesses that use Microsoft products. Rackspace also offers a range of security features such as anti-spam and anti-virus protection. Prices start at $2.99 per user per month.

5. FastMail

FastMail is a reliable email hosting provider that offers a range of email hosting options to businesses and individuals. FastMail offers excellent security and privacy features such as two-factor authentication and email encryption. FastMail also provides excellent customer support and a simple web-based interface. Prices start at $5 per user per month.

Choosing the right email hosting provider is essential for any business.
