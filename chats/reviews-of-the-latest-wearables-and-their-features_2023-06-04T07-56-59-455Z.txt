Samsung Galaxy Watch 3 is an all-rounder smartwatch designed for people who want to stay updated, connected, and healthy. Apple Watch Series 6 is one of the most popular smartwatches on the market. The Series 6 comes with an always-on display, blood oxygen monitoring, GPS tracking, and ECG monitoring.
