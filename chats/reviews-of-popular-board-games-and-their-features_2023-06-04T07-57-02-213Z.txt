Are you looking for a fun and engaging way to spend your free time? Then why not try playing some board games? This classic form of entertainment has seen a resurgence in popularity in recent years, and there are now countless games to choose from, catering to a range of tastes and preferences.

To help you navigate this overwhelming selection, we have compiled a list of the top board games available in 2021, along with reviews of their features and gameplay.

##1. Settlers of Catan

Settlers of Catan is a classic board game that still holds up today as one of the most popular games around. This game is a great introduction to the world of strategy board games, as it is easy to learn but challenging to master. It’s also highly replayable, as there are different random tiles and scenarios to keep the game fresh every time you play.

The goal of Settlers of Catan is to use your resources and trade with other players to build a civilization on the island of Catan. The game is won by being the first player to reach a certain number of victory points.

##2. Gloomhaven

Gloomhaven is a massive, immersive board game that takes you on a journey through a fantasy world filled with monsters, treasures, and quests. The gameplay is a deep and complex, and the game can take up to 100 hours to complete, but it is well worth the effort.

The game revolves around tactical combat, and players must work together to defeat monsters and complete objectives. Gloomhaven also has elements of role-playing games, as players can choose their characters, develop their abilities, and make decisions that affect the storyline.

##3. Pandemic

Pandemic is a cooperative game that puts players in the roles of scientists and medical professionals working to stop the spread of a deadly disease. The game is challenging and highly addictive, as each playthrough is different and presents new challenges.

The game is won by finding a cure for all four diseases before they spread too far and become uncontrollable. Pandemic is a great choice for players who enjoy working together and using strategy to solve puzzles.

##4. Ticket to Ride

Ticket to Ride is a fun and fast-paced game that is perfect for players of all ages and skill levels. The game is easy to learn but has plenty of strategic depth to keep it interesting.

The goal of Ticket to Ride is to create train routes between cities in a giant rail network, scoring points for each completed route. The game also introduces a little bit of luck, as players draw train cards and destination tickets from a pile.

##5. Scythe

Scythe is a classic 4X strategy game that pits players against each other in a battle for resources and territory. The game is set in an alternate history version of Europe, where industrial mechs roam the countryside and nations vie for power.

The gameplay is complex and deep, as players must balance resources, territory, and military power to emerge victorious. Scythe also offers a range of different factions, each with their own unique abilities and advantages.

##Conclusion

These five board games represent some of the best and most popular games available today. Whether you’re a fan of strategic combat, cooperative play, or just casual fun, there’s something on this list that will capture your attention.

So gather your friends, break out the snacks, and get ready to spend hours of fun and entertainment playing some of the best board games of 2021!
