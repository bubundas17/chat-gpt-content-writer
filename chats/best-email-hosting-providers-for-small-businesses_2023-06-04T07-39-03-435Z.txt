TITLE: Best Email Hosting Providers for Small Businesses - Top 5 Options in 2021 
META_DESCRIPTION: Find out the top 5 email hosting providers for small businesses in 2021. Learn about their features, pricing, and benefits. 
TAGS: small business, email hosting, providers, top 5, features, pricing, benefits 
CATEGORIES: Reviews, Small Businesses 

Email hosting is critical for small businesses, but finding the right provider can be a challenge. While many web hosting companies offer email hosting as part of their core service, issues like subpar spam filters, restrictive storage quotas, and poor uptime can be concerns. Thankfully, there are dedicated email hosting providers that offer superior performance and security for small businesses. In this article, we will discuss the top 5 email hosting providers for small businesses in 2021.

## 1. Zoho Mail

Zoho Mail is one of the top email hosting providers on the market, offering a variety of features for small businesses. It supports custom domains and offers a robust webmail client, along with mobile access via iOS and Android apps. Zoho Mail includes spam protection, email filters, and a 30-day email recovery feature. It offers a free plan for up to 5 users, while its premium plan with unlimited storage starts at just $1 per user per month.

## 2. Google Workspace

Google Workspace, formerly known as G Suite, is a cloud-based productivity and collaboration tool that includes email hosting for businesses. It offers Gmail, Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, and other productivity tools, along with custom domain support and mobile access. Google Workspace features robust spam filters, email routing rules, and 24/7 customer support. Its pricing starts at $6 per user per month.

## 3. Office 365

Office 365 by Microsoft is another top email hosting provider, with a wide range of features for small businesses. It includes cloud-based versions of Microsoft Office apps, Exchange Online for email hosting, and online storage via OneDrive. Office 365 offers hybrid deployment options, so you can link it with your existing on-premises Exchange Server. It also includes spam filters, email encryption, and a 99.9% uptime guarantee. Its pricing starts at $5 per user per month.

## 4. Bluehost

Bluehost is a popular web hosting provider that also offers email hosting for small businesses. It allows custom domains, IMAP/POP3 support, and works with Microsoft Outlook. Its email hosting plans come with unlimited storage, spam protection, and email filters. Bluehost's Basic plan starts at $2.95 per month, with email hosting included.

## 5. Intermedia Exchange Email

Intermedia Exchange Email is a dedicated email hosting provider that offers a variety of features for small businesses. It includes custom domain support, webmail access, and mobile access via iOS and Android apps. Intermedia's Exchange Email also comes with robust spam protection, email archiving, and a 99.999% uptime guarantee. Its pricing starts at $7.99 per user per month.

In conclusion, small businesses have many options when it comes to email hosting providers. While web hosts may offer email hosting, dedicated email hosting providers offer superior performance and security. Zoho Mail, Google Workspace, Office 365, Bluehost, and Intermedia Exchange Email are great options for small businesses in 2021. When choosing an email hosting provider, consider factors like cost, features, and security to ensure the best solution for your business's needs.
