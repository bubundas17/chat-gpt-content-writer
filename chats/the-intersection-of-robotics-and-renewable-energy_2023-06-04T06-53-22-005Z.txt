One of the main advantages of solar energy is its almost limitless potential. Advances in robotics have brought sophisticated automation tools to the renewable energy industry. Automated systems can also improve the accuracy in positioning of solar panels. A single miscalculation can result in up to a 15% decrease in panel efficiency. Robotics has made it possible to automate the process of positioning and adjusting solar panels to receive the optimal amount of sunlight, thereby maximizing the energy they produce.

## Reducing Costs with Autonomous Microgrids

The role of microgrids has been increasing in the renewable energy sector and robotics is playing an important role in their evolution. In a microgrid, energy production and usage are closely coordinated to reduce waste and optimize the distribution of renewable energy.

Robots and automation technologies offer fast and accurate solutions to improve the performance of energy storage systems in microgrids. Automation also enables smart charging by allowing the battery in an electric vehicle to be charged during non-peak periods and used as part of the energy source during peak hours. This represents a significant cost-saving approach for those looking to make the shift to renewable energy.
