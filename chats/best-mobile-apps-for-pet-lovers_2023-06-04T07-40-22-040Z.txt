Today, we'll take a closer look at some of the top mobile apps for pet lovers that you need to check out in 2021.

1. PetCoach
PetCoach is a free app that provides expert advice and personalized answers to all your pet-related questions. From nutrition to exercise, you can get advice from veterinarians and pet care experts on a wide range of topics.

2. Rover
Rover is a pet sitting and dog walking app that connects pet owners with trusted local sitters and walkers. You can easily book a dog walker or pet sitter, get photo updates, and even pay through the app.

3. Chewy
Chewy is an online pet store that offers thousands of products for your furry friends. With the Chewy app, you can easily shop for pet food, treats, toys, and more, and have them delivered right to your doorstep.

4. MyPetEd
MyPetEd is a pet training app that offers step-by-step guides for teaching your pet new tricks and behaviors. The app includes videos, articles, and custom training plans based on your pet's breed and age.

5. Pet First Aid
Accidents can happen, and when they do, it's important to be prepared. Pet First Aid is a comprehensive app that provides information on emergency situations and how to care for your pet in case of an injury or illness.

6. Whistle
Whistle is a pet tracker that allows you to keep tabs on your pet's location and activity levels. The device attaches to your pet's collar and syncs with the app, so you can monitor their movements and get alerts if they stray too far.

7. PetDesk
PetDesk is a pet health app that helps you keep track of your pet's vaccinations, medication schedules, and upcoming vet appointments. You can also order prescription refills and set reminders for important dates.

These are just a few of the top mobile apps for pet lovers in 2021. From training and exercise to nutrition and health, these apps have got you covered. Download them today and start taking better care of your furry friends.
