TITLE: Top Email Management Tools to Streamline Your Workflow
META_DESCRIPTION: Discover the best email management tools for increasing productivity and streamlining your email workflow. From AI-powered email assistants to task management and scheduling tools, we've got you covered.
TAGS: email management, productivity, workflow, AI assistants, task management, scheduling, organization
CATEGORIES: Productivity, Software Development, Emerging Technologies

Email has become an integral part of our daily lives and work. It's almost impossible to imagine a world without email. However, managing your inbox can be overwhelming and time-consuming, leading to a decline in productivity. Fortunately, there are a plethora of email management tools available that can help you streamline your workflow and increase productivity. In this post, we've curated a list of the best email management tools that can assist you in managing your inbox more efficiently.

## 1. Boomerang

Boomerang is a popular email management tool that allows you to schedule your emails to be sent later, track responses and set reminders for follow-ups. This tool is great for individuals or teams that frequently communicate with clients or stakeholders across different time zones. Boomerang is also available as a mobile app, making it easy to schedule and track emails from your mobile device.

## 2. Clean Email

Clean Email is an email management tool that automatically organizes your inbox by grouping newsletters, notifications, and other unwanted emails into specific folders. With Clean Email, you can quickly scan your inbox and focus on the emails that matter. This tool also allows you to easily unsubscribe from unwanted emails, saving you time and reducing clutter.

## 3. SaneBox

SaneBox uses machine learning algorithms to sort your emails into different categories, such as important, spam, newsletters, etc. This tool also allows you to snooze emails and set reminders, keeping your inbox clutter-free and organized. SaneBox integrates with popular email clients, such as Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail, making it easy to use with your existing email setup.

## 4. Hiver

Hiver is an email management tool designed for teams that collaborate on email. With Hiver, you can share your inbox with other team members, assign tasks, and track progress. This tool is ideal for businesses that receive a lot of customer support emails and need to manage them effectively. Hiver also integrates with popular project management tools, such as Asana and Trello.

## 5. Grammarly

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that checks your emails for spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors. This tool is perfect for individuals who frequently communicate via email and want to make a good impression. Grammarly provides real-time suggestions and corrections, ensuring that your emails are polished and professional.

## 6. Unroll.me

Unroll.me is an email management tool that enables you to unsubscribe from unwanted emails in bulk. With Unroll.me, you can quickly unsubscribe from newsletters, promotions, and other unwanted emails, reducing inbox clutter and saving you time. This tool also allows you to combine multiple subscription emails into one email, making it easier to manage.

In conclusion, managing your email can be a daunting task, but with these email management tools, it doesn't have to be. From scheduling emails to tracking responses, AI-powered assistants to task management tools, these email management tools can help streamline your workflow and increase productivity. Give them a try and see which ones work best for you and your team.
