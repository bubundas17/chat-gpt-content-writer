To enable live chat support on your website, you must install the tracking code provided by your live chat software provider. This code allows the chat software to identify visitors on your website and start a conversation. Typically, the code is added to the header or footer section of your website.

Step 4: Train customer service agents
Before launching live chat support on your website, it’s important to train customer service agents on how to effectively use the software and provide top-notch support. This includes responding promptly, using pre-written responses, and providing solutions to customer problems.

Step 5: Launch live chat support
Once you have completed the previous steps, it’s time to launch live chat support on your website!
