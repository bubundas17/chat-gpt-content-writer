## Instacart

Instacart is one of the most popular online grocery delivery services in the United States, serving more than 5,500 cities and partnering with over 600 national and local retailers, including Whole Foods, Safeway, Costco, and Kroger. Instacart offers same-day delivery of groceries and everyday essentials from local stores, with delivery fees starting at $3.99.

Instacart offers a subscription service, Instacart Express, which offers unlimited free deliveries on orders over $35. Instacart Express members also enjoy reduced service fees, no busy pricing fees during peak delivery hours, and the option to shop from multiple stores in one order.

## Amazon Fresh

Amazon Fresh is a grocery delivery service offered by Amazon, available exclusively to Prime members. Amazon Fresh offers a selection of groceries, including fresh produce, meat, seafood, and household items, with no minimum order amount and free delivery on orders over $35.

Amazon Fresh offers a free 30-day trial for Prime members, after which it costs $14.99 per month as an add-on to your Prime membership.

## Walmart Grocery

Walmart Grocery is an online grocery ordering and delivery service offered by Walmart. Walmart Grocery allows you to order groceries online and pick them up at your local Walmart store or have them delivered to your doorstep. Walmart Grocery offers same-day delivery for a fee starting at $7.95.

Walmart Grocery offers a free trial for its Delivery Unlimited service, which offers unlimited deliveries for a flat annual fee of $98 or $12.95 per month.

## Shipt

Shipt is a membership-based grocery delivery service that partners with retailers like Target, Costco, and CVS Pharmacy to deliver groceries, household essentials, and more. Shipt members can place an order through their app and receive same-day delivery, with prices varying by retailer. Shipt charges a delivery fee of $10 per order, with no minimum order amount.

Shipt offers a free four-week trial for new members, after which it costs $99 per year or $14 per month.

## FreshDirect

FreshDirect is an online grocer that offers next-day delivery of groceries, meal kits, and household items to customers in the Northeastern United States. FreshDirect offers a wide selection of fresh and organic produce, meat, seafood, and grocery items, with delivery fees ranging from $5.99 to $15.99.

Unlike other services, FreshDirect does not offer a free trial option, but it does offer regular promotions and discounts for new and existing customers.

Choosing the best online grocery delivery service depends on your personal preferences and needs. Each service offers unique features and benefits, from same-day delivery to subscription options and free trials.
