## Starting Fresh: How to Make a Successful Transition to a Career in Tech

If you are looking to start a new career in tech, you are not alone. In 2021, the tech industry is growing faster than ever before, and with that growth comes an influx of job opportunities for people of all backgrounds and skill levels. However, transitioning to a new career can be challenging, especially if you don't have experience in the tech industry.

### 1. Determine Your Goals and Interests

### 2. Build Your Skills
After you've determined your goals and interests, start building your skills. Identify the skills necessary for the role you're interested in and work on developing them. ### 3. Network, Network, Network
