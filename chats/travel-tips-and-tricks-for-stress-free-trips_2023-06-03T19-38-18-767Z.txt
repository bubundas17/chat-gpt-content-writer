5. Bring Entertainment
Long flights or layovers can be boring and stressful. Bringing entertainment, such as a book, music, or a movie, can make the time go by faster and make your trip more enjoyable.

6. Book Accommodation in Advance
Booking your accommodation in advance can save you a lot of stress. Not only does it give you time to research and find the best deal, but it also ensures that you have a place to stay when you arrive.

7. Use Technology
