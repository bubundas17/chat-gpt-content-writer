- The sensitive nature of government communications

- The high volume of email communication

- The need for compliance with government regulations

- The risk of cyber attacks and data breaches

1. Use a Hosted Email Service: Government agencies should consider using a hosted email service that provides advanced security and compliance features. Government agencies should use an email hosting solution that provides two-factor authentication to secure their email communication.
