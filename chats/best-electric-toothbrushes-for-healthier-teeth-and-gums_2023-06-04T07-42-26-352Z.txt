- Promote better brushing technique: Electric toothbrushes typically have built-in timers and pressure sensors to help you brush for the appropriate amount of time and with the right amount of pressure. This can help promote better brushing technique and reduce the risk of overbrushing.

- Offer customizable features: Many electric toothbrushes come with customizable settings, such as brush head replacements, cleaning modes, and pressure levels, to cater to your specific needs.
