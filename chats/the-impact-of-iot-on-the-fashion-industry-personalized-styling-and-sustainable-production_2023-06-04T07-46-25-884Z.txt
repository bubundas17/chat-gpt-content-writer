Personalized Styling

Sustainable Production

IoT is also helping to make fashion production more sustainable. Traditional clothing production methods are notoriously resource-intensive, with massive amounts of water, energy, and chemicals used in the process. IoT technologies are allowing designers and manufacturers to create more sustainable clothing by monitoring the supply chain and managing resources more efficiently.

For example, one company, called Evrnu, uses a technology that breaks down cotton garment waste into a pulp, which is then transformed into a renewable fiber. This process eliminates the need for water and uses only a fraction of the energy required by traditional manufacturing methods, resulting in a dramatically reduced carbon footprint.

Conclusion

The impact of IoT on the fashion industry is transforming how we buy and wear clothing. Personalized styling and sustainable production are becoming increasingly common as more companies adopt IoT technologies.
