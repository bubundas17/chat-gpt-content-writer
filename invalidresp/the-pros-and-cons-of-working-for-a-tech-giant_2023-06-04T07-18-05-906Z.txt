PROS

2. Compensation and Benefits: Tech giants are widely regarded for offering some of the best compensation packages in the industry. Many of these companies provide their employees with high salaries, bonuses and other benefits like health insurance, paid vacation, and retirement plans.

CONS

2. Work-Life Balance: Many employees of tech giants often report a poor work-life balance. The demands and expectations of their job can consume their time and energy, leaving their personal lives and time outside of work on the backburner.
